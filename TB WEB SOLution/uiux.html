Our Design Process

1

User Research & Persona Mapping

We start by understanding your users through comprehensive research, creating detailed personas and user journey maps to guide our design decisions.

2

Wireframing

Low-fidelity wireframes establish the structure and flow of your interface, focusing on functionality and user experience before visual design.

3

High-Fidelity Mockups

Detailed visual designs that bring your interface to life with colors, typography, imagery, and branding elements perfectly aligned.

4

Prototyping

Interactive prototypes allow you to experience the user flow and test functionality before development begins.

5

Handoff & Developer Collaboration

Seamless design-to-development transition with detailed specifications, assets, and ongoing support throughout implementation.
